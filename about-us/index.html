About Sip Sap
Spring – the season of the year, when melting snow fills the roots of each, awakened tree with vitamins and minerals. It is the birth of sap – healthy and unique drink the nature gifts us every year.

SipSap - taste the fresh blooming forest all year round!

How do we collect birch sap?

For centuries, the birch forests have been tapped for their unique tree water - sap. The traditions of tapping remain the same even nowadays as well as the fresh energy blast each time you sip a sap.

SipSap is being collected at its best quality – in the very beginning of March from 2 to 4 weeks in certified and organic forest areas. A small hole is being made in the trunk of healthy birch, having drop after drop fill the tanks. Almost 100 liters of sap can be collected form one birch over the season.

Although the tapping season is short, certified technology allows us to serve you fresh sap all year round!

How do we prepare birch tree sap?

It takes a long way for the drop to reach the customers. Profound checks have to be made and requirements met for the sap to find its way to the shelves. To ensure irreproachability – flavour, taste and smell must be laboratory tested. It is sensitive and squeamish product that starts to perish soon, however, improved technologies allow us to keep sap fresh, saving all valuable qualities. SipSap is being sterilized and safely closed without any preservatives.

The quality of birch tree sap

Lithuania is rich of birch trees, therefore we can collect a large amount of sap locally. We call ourselves the perfectionists of taste presenting and always searching for the variety of flavours in order for each of you to find the favourite one.
