Birch Water

Birch Water is filtered and enrichted with minerals by the Birch Tree itself.

 

Birch Water is made from Birch Sap which is extracted from the Birch Tree durings short time ( from 2 to 4 weeks ) at the break of winter and spring when the Sap moves intensvelys.

 

A small hole is drilled in the Tree trunk and Sap is collected, No harm to the Tree is done.

 

Sap is watery, slightly sweet liquid.

 

Rich in vitamins, minerals, amino acids,enzymers, phytoncides, essential oils and sugars.
